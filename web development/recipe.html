How do you make a cappuccino?

Ingredients

one-fourth cup instant coffee powder
one-fourth cup sugar
3 tbsp water
milk as required

Instructions

  1. Firstly, in a large bowl take one-forth cup instant coffee powder,one-fourth cup sugar and 3 tbsp water.
  2. Mix well to dissolve the sugar.
  3. Using a hand beater, beat the mixture. You can also beat it by using a whisk or blender.
  4. Beat for 5 minutes or until the mixture turns frothy and lightens in colour.
  5. Make sure the mixture is thick and creamy. you can store this mixture for a week in an airtight container by refrigerating.
  6. To prepare the cappuccino, heat 2 cups of milk.
  7. Boil the milk and whisk it to make it frothy.
  8. In a cup, add 1 tbsp of cappuccino mixture and top it with a cup of frothy milk and mix gently. To make it stronger, add another tbsp of cappuccino mixture and mix gently.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
